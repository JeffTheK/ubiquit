# Operators

### Arithmetic

* `+` - addition
* `-` - subtraction
* `*` - multiplication
* `/` - division
* `%` - modulus
* `**` - exponent



### Comparison

* `==` equals
* `!=` not equals
* `>` greater
* `<` less
* `>=` greater or equals
* `<=` less or equals



### Assignment

* `=` simple assignment
* `+=` add and assign
* `-=` subtract and assign
